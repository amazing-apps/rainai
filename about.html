About us

Welcome to RainAI – the weather app that’s always one step ahead.

We know how frustrating it is to get caught in the rain without an umbrella, or to cancel plans because of a forecast that turned out wrong. That’s why we built RainAI: a smarter, more reliable way to know what the sky has in store.

Our secret? Accuracy and timing. With real-time rain alerts, RainAI notifies you the very moment showers are about to begin—so you can plan your day with confidence. From minute-by-minute precision to hourly and 10-day forecasts, our technology is constantly evolving to deliver the clearest picture of the weather, no matter where you are.

And we’re just getting started. Soon, RainAI will bring you even more smart notifications and helpful insights, making it your go-to weather companion for every season and every adventure.

With RainAI, you don’t just check the forecast—you stay ahead of it.
